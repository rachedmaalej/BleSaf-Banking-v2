ALTERNATIVE 1

Dark Command Strip

Full-width dark bar inspired by cockpit status displays. Health score as a color-coded badge on the far left, KPIs as bright-text segments separated by subtle dividers, quick actions as ghost-bordered pills on the right. High contrast for at-a-glance scanning in a busy branch environment.

ALTERNATIVE 2

Metric Card Grid

Each KPI lives in its own mini-card, forming a uniform grid. The health score gets a double-width card with the gauge. Quick actions sit in a compact toolbar card on the right. Easier to scan than a dense strip, and each card can independently flash when values cross thresholds.

ALTERNATIVE 3

Split-Level with Progress Bar

Two distinct rows. Top row: large health score left, three primary KPIs center (waiting, wait time, served), and quick actions right. Bottom row: a full-width dual progress bar showing Capacity and SLA as side-by-side colored tracks. The progress bar gives an instant visual read of the two most actionable metrics.

ALTERNATIVE 4

Centered Command

The health score is the undeniable focal point — a large radial gauge centered at the top, flanked symmetrically by KPIs on each side (3 left, 2 right + actions). This layout draws the eye to the single most important number first, then spreads detail outward. Quick actions are icon-only circles along the bottom edge for a clean, minimal footprint.

ALTERNATIVE 5

Traffic Light Dashboard

Inspired by traffic-light signaling: a prominent color-coded status banner on the left communicates overall branch health at a glance (red/amber/green). KPIs are organized into a compact table-like layout with micro-progress indicators inline. The bold left accent strip changes color based on the health score, creating an unmistakable visual signal visible even from across the room.

Comparison Summary

Alternative Layout Health Score Best For Trade-off
1. Dark Command Strip Single row, dark background Circular badge High-contrast scanning, busy environments Darker style may contrast with white page body
2. Metric Card Grid Even card grid + action row Mini semicircular gauge Equal weight to all KPIs, clean separation Takes more vertical space than a strip
3. Split-Level + Progress 2 rows: KPIs top, progress bars bottom Square badge with label SLA/Capacity tracking at a glance via bars Two-row layout uses slightly more height
4. Centered Command Symmetrical, gauge center Large centered gauge (focal) Health score as the primary decision signal KPIs slightly further from actions
5. Traffic Light Side accent strip + KPI grid Radial ring with color strip Instant status visibility, per-KPI micro-bars Left strip may feel asymmetric
