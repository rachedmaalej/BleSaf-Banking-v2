SG Aesthetic Principles Applied

  • Red sparingly: SG Red (#E9041E) only appears for the most important action button, not for decorative elements
  • Monochrome foundation: Black, white, and grays dominate - creating a sophisticated, professional atmosphere
  • Service colors as accents: Thin lines or small dots indicate service type - never large color blocks
  • Generous white space: Content breathes, creating a sense of premium quality
  • Light typography: Thin font weights (200-300) for large numbers create an executive, refined look
  • Flat design: Minimal shadows, clean borders, no gradients (except logo placeholder)
